Why Use /Atomics?

Philanthropic Focus

We focus on the value of donating blood. We do this by adding accountability to the clinics who collect your blood. You'll be able to see exactly where your blood goes.

Rewards

Get rewarded for your contributions! We are partnering with organizations to help you with your everday costs.

Transparency

Using the Ethereum Blockchain, you'll know exactly where your contribution goes. This prevents any backdoor deals and let's you see where you or your loved one's contribution goes.
